Serves: 4
Prep time: 0 hours 10 minutes
Cook time: 1 hours 00 minutes

Ingredients

  • 1tsp vegetable oil
  • 2 Carrots, finely diced
  • 1 Onion, finely dices
  • 1 Celery stick, finely diced
  • 2 Cloves of Garlic, crushed
  • 400g lean Minced Beef
  • 400g tin of Chopped Tomatoes
  • 2tbsp Tomato Puree
  • 300ml Beef Stock
  • 1tsp Worcestershire Sauce
  • 2tsp Italian Herbs
  • Salt and Pepper

Method

  1. Heat the vegetable oil in a large pan on a medium heat, then add the carrots, onion and celery and cook for five to ten minutes to soften, stirring occasionally
  2. Add the crushed garlic and cook for 2 more minutes, stiffing occasionally
  3. Add the minced beef to the pan, breaking it up with a wooden spoon and cook until browned off
  4. Add the chopped tomatoes, tomato puree, beef stock, worcestershire sauce and italian herbs and stir. Add a pinch of salt and pepper then stir through
  5. Bring to a gentle simmer and cook for 30 minutes
